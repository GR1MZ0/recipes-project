Lemonade

Description

Oh and of cause no meal is complete without a beverage in ths case it will be lemonade for our drink of choice made the limes or what ever citrus fruit of your choice

Ingredients

  • lime,lemons,grapefruit
  • sugar
  • flavored essence
  • 1 jug of water

Steps

  1. slice your fruit of choice into a jug.
  2. Put a strainer over the jug so the seeds won't fall into the jug.
  3. Add enough water to the jug an mix it with a spoon.
  4. Add some sugar to the mixture and mix it taste it to make sure if is sweet enough.
  5. Add two drops of flavored essence an mix it into the lemonade.
